While you are here, there are a few things to keep in mind.

  • As of Summer 2016, this is an internal ESH tool only. Please utilize to guide your research and findings, but do not share this link with external stakeholders.

  • Data is updated Friday morning of each week. Please note the data of the last data pull below.

  • The selection you make in the upper-right hand corner for state and data cleanliness carry through for every tab. To adjust, please re-filter.

  • To see more information about a particular district, please visit our IRT tool

  • The visualizations are not designed for final use, but you can save and export them by right-clicking.

  • The data exports are included to help you see the details and data behind the visualization and are as of the data of the last data pull.
